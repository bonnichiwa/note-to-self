Little musings addressed to the invisible voice in my head

By: Bonnie Pham

Shimla, India, December 2017

It's chillier than I thought it would be, and I definitely didn't bring thick enough sweaters. To keep myself warm I've been drinking "masala chai" here everyday.

Not that I mind.

There's a lot of initimate shops here, one or two tables seating max. It's a nice change. No big chains, no noise. Just good tea and good conversations.

Sometimes there's not even tea. Grab a friend, kick back, and have a chat.
